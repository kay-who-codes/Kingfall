Kingfall

Avoid being the last player holding a King when the deck runs out!

Player 1 26 cards
Player 2 26 cards
Player 1's turn. Select a card to play.

Game Rules

  • Goal: Avoid holding the King when the deck runs out.
  • Deal the deck evenly between players.
  • Players take turns forcing the other to draw cards (play a 4 = opponent draws 4, etc.).
  • Face cards have special effects:
    • Jack: Reverse draw direction.
    • Queen: Skip opponent's turn.
    • King: Must be passed to the opponent if possible.
  • Last player holding a King when cards run out loses.

Player 1 Wins!

Player 2 was left holding the King!
